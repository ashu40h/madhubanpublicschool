Our Curriculum

We follow the CBSE curriculum with innovative teaching methodologies to ensure comprehensive learning.

CBSE Curriculum

Madhuban Public School is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. We follow the NCERT curriculum which is designed to provide a strong foundation in core subjects while encouraging critical thinking and creativity.

Our curriculum is structured to develop students' cognitive, emotional, and social skills through a balanced approach to academics and co-curricular activities.

Class Range

We offer education from Nursery to Class 12, covering all age groups and developmental stages. Our programs are designed to meet the specific needs of each age group while maintaining continuity in learning.

  • • Pre-Primary: Nursery, LKG, UKG (Ages 3-5)
  • • Primary: Classes 1-5 (Ages 6-10)
  • • Middle School: Classes 6-8 (Ages 11-13)
  • • Secondary: Classes 9-10 (Ages 14-15)
  • • Senior Secondary: Classes 11-12 (Ages 16-17)

Teaching Methodology

Our innovative teaching methods ensure effective learning and student engagement.

Interactive Learning

Student-centered approach with interactive sessions, group discussions, and hands-on activities to enhance understanding.

Smart Classrooms

Technology-integrated classrooms with digital boards, projectors, and educational software for enhanced learning experience.

Assessment & Evaluation

Continuous and comprehensive evaluation system with regular assessments, projects, and practical examinations.

Individual Attention

Small class sizes with personalized attention to ensure every student receives the support they need to excel.

Practical Learning

Emphasis on practical applications through laboratory work, field trips, and real-world problem-solving activities.

Remedial Classes

Additional support classes for students who need extra help in specific subjects to ensure no child is left behind.

Co-Curricular Activities

We believe in holistic development through a wide range of co-curricular activities.

Sports & Physical Education

Cultural Activities

Music, dance, drama, art, and craft activities to nurture creativity and cultural awareness.

Science & Technology

Science clubs, robotics, computer programming, and technology-based learning activities.

Literary Activities

Community Service

Social service activities, environmental awareness programs, and community outreach initiatives.

Leadership Programs

Student council, prefect system, and leadership development programs to build leadership qualities.

Special Programs

Career Guidance

Competitive Exam Preparation

Special coaching for various competitive examinations including JEE, NEET, and other entrance tests.

Language Development

English language enhancement programs and communication skills development for better expression.

Environmental Education
