About Our School

Madhuban Public School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Kuchpura, Karnal for over 25 years. We are committed to providing a nurturing environment where students can grow academically, socially, and personally.

Our comprehensive curriculum, experienced faculty, and modern facilities ensure that every student receives the best possible education to prepare them for the challenges of tomorrow.
