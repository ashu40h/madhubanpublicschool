Admission Procedure

Follow these simple steps to secure admission for your child at Madhuban Public School.

Step 1: Application Submission

Submit the completed application form along with required documents and application fee.

Step 2: Document Verification

Our admission team will verify all submitted documents and contact you for any clarifications.

Step 3: Interaction/Assessment

Students and parents will be invited for an interaction session to understand the school's values and expectations.

Step 4: Admission Confirmation

Upon successful completion of the process, admission will be confirmed and fee payment details will be provided.

Age Criteria

Admission is open to students based on the following age criteria for the academic year 2025-26.

Nursery

Age: 3+ years as on 31st March 2025

LKG

Age: 4+ years as on 31st March 2025

UKG

Age: 5+ years as on 31st March 2025

Class 1

Age: 6+ years as on 31st March 2025

Class 2-5

Age: 7-10 years as on 31st March 2025

Class 6-12

Age: 11-17 years as on 31st March 2025

Documents Required

Please ensure you have the following documents ready for the admission process.

Birth Certificate

Original birth certificate issued by municipal authority or hospital.

Transfer Certificate

Transfer certificate from previous school (for Class 1 onwards).

Mark Sheets

Previous academic year mark sheets and report cards.

Photographs

Recent passport size photographs of student and parents.

Address Proof

Valid address proof (Aadhaar card, electricity bill, etc.).

Medical Certificate

Medical fitness certificate from a registered medical practitioner.

Fee Structure

Transparent and affordable fee structure for quality education. Contact us for detailed fee information.

Admission Fee

One-time admission fee (non-refundable) as per school policy.

Tuition Fee

Monthly tuition fee covering academic instruction and basic facilities.

Development Fee

Annual development fee for infrastructure and facility maintenance.

Transport Fee

Optional transport fee for students using school bus service.

Examination Fee

Annual examination fee for CBSE board examinations and internal assessments.

Other Charges

Additional charges for uniforms, books, and extracurricular activities as applicable.
